How it works

1. Sign up

Sign up with your email and password. You can also sign in with your Google account.

2. Copy code to /profile

A key feature is imploring users to visit your personal site. Copy this code block into /profile on your own website.

<iframe src="http://127.0.0.1:8000/Linkfeed/profile/Eli%20Richmond/" style="border:none;height:100%;width:100%;"></iframe>

3. Copy code to /feed

Just like step 2, copy this code block into /feed.

<iframe src="http://127.0.0.1:8000/Linkfeed/profile/Eli%20Richmond/" style="border:none;height:100%;width:100%;"></iframe>

Feel free to shoot me an email. I'd be happy to help :)
